XI

Prompt Book · NanaBanana 2 · Alumni Executive Club

⚠️ Обязательное правило генерации для всех образов

В каждом образе акцидентные цвета фиолетовый (#7700FF) и оранжевый (#FF4F12) должны быть зашиты в композицию — даже при преимущественно монохромной гамме. Violet: доминирует (свечение, отблеск, деталь). Orange: не более 10–15% кадра (точечный акцент, блик, разделитель). Фон всегда тёмный. Никакой военной символики, оружия, флагов, кораблей военно-морского флота.

Промты на четыре образа

PROMPT 01 · ICEBREAKER / FLOTILLA · NanaBanana 2 · EN only
Abstract dark industrial composition. NO ship, NO vessel, NO military imagery. Core element: a sharp angular wedge form (12–15 degree cutting angle) slicing through a dark matte surface — like the bow geometry of an icebreaker, abstracted into pure form. Material: brushed dark steel or polished anthracite. Matte finish. Light: single cold directional source, side angle, creates deep relief shadows. Colors: background #0A0A0A, violet #7700FF as thin cold light leaking through the cut — precise, electric. Orange #FF4F12 as a single narrow streak at the tip of the wedge — max 10% of frame. No warm tones. No water literally. No neon effects. Mood: strategic calm, unstoppable force, systemic power, engineering precision. Style reference: dark fine-art industrial photography meets engineering diagram. Prohibited: no ships, no military vessels, no flags, no weapons, no warm colors, no people, no text, no logos, no decorative elements. Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 1:1. High resolution. Matte surface, no gloss.
PROMPT 02 · NORTH / ICE / UNSINKABLE · NanaBanana 2 · EN only
Abstract monochrome arctic texture composition. Scene option A: extreme close-up of dense multi-year ice surface — structural crack patterns, compressed layers visible, geological solidity. Scene option B: cold polar horizon — dark water, silver-grey sky, no sun warmth. Material: ice as a load-bearing structure — not fragile, ancient and dense. Light: diffused polar light, no directional shadows, silver-steel palette. Colors: monochrome dominant (#0A0A0A, #2A2A2A, #AAAAAA). Violet #7700FF as a faint deep reflection within the ice layers. Orange #FF4F12 as a minimal cold glint at one edge — max 8% of frame. Mood: endurance, discipline, silent power, unsinkable force. Style reference: arctic documentary photography, scientific expedition aesthetic. Prohibited: no warm tones, no sunlight warmth, no gloss, no people, no polar bears or animals, no text, no logos, no decorative elements. Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 3:4. Matte finish.
PROMPT 03 · WIRES / CABLES / NETWORKS · NanaBanana 2 · EN only
Dark industrial composition: a dense, massive bundle of cables and wires. CRITICAL: many wires — not one cable, not a detail — a thick, heavy, industrial bundle of dozens of cables running together. Dense, saturated, filling the frame with the weight of infrastructure. Material: insulated industrial cables, rubber or plastic coating, worn surface. Composition: the bundle runs diagonally or slightly curved across the frame. Close-up or medium close-up — cables fill 60–70% of the frame. Light: single cold industrial light source. Deep shadows between cable layers. Colors: dark base #0A0A0A. The cable bundle: near-black or very dark grey. Violet #7700FF: thin electric glow along one cable edge or between layers. Orange #FF4F12: a single cable or detail highlighted — max 10–15% of frame. Mood: infrastructure as evidence of massive work done, quiet industrial pride, belonging through physical trace. Style: macro industrial photography, documentary infrastructure aesthetic. Prohibited: no digital network diagrams, no topology lines, no abstract patterns, no single wire, no warm colors, no people, no text, no logos. Aspect ratio: 1:1. Matte surface.
PROMPT 04 · METAL / REBAR / STRUCTURE · NanaBanana 2 · EN only
Macro industrial texture — dark metal as the main subject. Scene option A: brushed dark steel surface — fine directional grain visible, matte finish, not reflective. Scene option B: rebar or structural metal — raw, industrial, load-bearing. Close-up: texture fills the frame, structural quality is the only message. Material: anthracite steel, cast iron, or reinforced concrete rebar. Matte surface — no gloss, no mirror reflections, no polished chrome. Light: single directional hard light, raking across the surface to reveal texture. Creates deep shadows in grooves and grain — maximum relief. Colors: monochrome dominant (#0A0A0A, #2A2A2A, #555555). Violet #7700FF: thin spectral light in a groove or edge detail. Orange #FF4F12: a single warm metal highlight at one point — max 8% of frame. Mood: engineering dignity, institutional authority, strength without ornament. Style reference: industrial macro photography, architectural detail photography. Prohibited: no gold, no copper, no warm metals, no gloss, no mirror surfaces, no decorative metalwork, no jewelry, no people, no text, no logos. Aspect ratio: 1:1. High detail, matte finish.

Мастер-промт и иерархия

Система иерархическая. Мастер-промт — постоянная база, описывает всю идентику. Add-on промты раскрывают конкретный формат. Всегда подключаются к мастер-промту. Все промты на латинице. Инструмент: NanaBanana 2.

Иерархия

1. Вставить МАСТЕР-ПРОМТ в начало сессии NanaBanana 2 (один раз) → 2. Добавить ADD-ON для нужного формата → 3. Заполнить поля [IN BRACKETS] — зоны ручного ввода

MASTER PROMPT · XI Alumni Executive Club · Rostelecom · NanaBanana 2
## VISUAL IDENTITY SYSTEM — XI Alumni Executive Club (Rostelecom) ### BRAND ESSENCE Closed premium executive club for former top management of Rostelecom. Symbol XI (Roman numeral 11) = 11 time zones = scale of people who built and maintained Russia's digital infrastructure. Not marketing — heraldic. Name in Latin: Alumni Executive. Full name: Alumni Executive Club Rostelecom. ### MANDATORY COLOR SYSTEM Background: #0A0A0A or #141414 (deep matte black) Accent 1 DOMINANT: Violet #7700FF — headlines, symbol, glowing details Accent 2 SECONDARY: Orange #FF4F12 — max 30% of violet usage per composition Supporting: monochrome only (#2A2A2A / #555555 / #AAAAAA / #FFFFFF) Gradient: #7700FF → #FF4F12 only for accent separators / active lines Rule: both violet AND orange must appear in every image composition. Additional colors: max 3% total in one layout. ### STYLE RULES — MANDATORY NO classic illustrations, decorative patterns, friendly mascots NO warm color palettes, bright colors, corporate blue gradients NO stock business photography, NO smiling teams, NO motivational posters NO military imagery — NO warships, NO weapons, NO military vessels YES: icebreaker aesthetic is civilian/engineering/scientific — not military YES: typography carries meaning (large headline + small caption contrast) YES: industrial materials: steel, ice, cables, dark paper YES: generous negative space — luxury of emptiness YES: single directional light, industrial shadows, matte textures ### FOUR IMAGE SYSTEM (abstract application, never literal) 01 ICEBREAKER/FLOTILLA: angular wedge form, cutting geometry, directional force 02 NORTH/ICE: monochrome arctic texture, dense ancient ice, silent endurance 03 WIRES/CABLES: massive dense bundle of cables — many wires, not one detail 04 METAL/REBAR: dark matte steel surface, structural material, texture as subject ### TYPOGRAPHY BASE (Figma/design reference) Font system: Rostelecom brand fonts as base. Key contrast: large headline (meaning) + small caption (context). ### BRAND TYPE XI = sub-brand of Rostelecom and HR brand. Public: XI mark without Rostelecom reference. Name: Alumni Executive (Latin). Internal: Alumni Executive Club Rostelecom / Liga XI.
